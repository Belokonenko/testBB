The Jamstack 2 comes equiped with both a Transmitter & Reciever.

The stereo transmitter allows you to

  • Connect with up to two other additional Jamstacks for INSANE volume & stereo effects.
  • Create a monitoring setup with 1 speaker facing you & up to 2 others facing your audience.
  • Use ultra low latency wireless headphones.
  • Connect your existing home audio setup to the Jamstack using a second receiver.
  • Connect your existing home audio setup to the Jamstack using a second receiver.
  • Connect to a wireless subwoofer.

The reciever allows you to

  • Operate the Jamstack completely wirelessly. This can be your acoustic guitar, violin, synthesiser or any other instrument with an audio output.
  • Bring in another instrument like a bass or synthesizer into your Jamstack 2.
  • Use your Jamstack for completely wireless home audio (up to 5.1 with a compatible sound bar)
